SIR SENGREEN LE

I am HEAVEN'S SWORD

Seven years ago,
I turned heavensward to look at
the one to become my darling
in the last of days, amidst
the LUNARIAN WAY, and the sway

of THE LAST of the LAST ELEVEN:

LIN XUE, THE WHITE STARLING,
and my darling, my darling,
though I promise to kill him
at world's end.

But until then...
